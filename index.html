About Me

Born and bread in the year 1991, in Accra Ghana, I was born into a family of six. My dream was to become an auto-technician because I saw my father do it and I loved it. In fact I love automobile even till now. Growing up, I realized I could make poeple laugh so I ventured into theatre and had my first degree in it. Through my theatre studies, I unearthed my communication talent and pusued it. Thus, I trained for radio and worked with the University of Ghana's campus radio station for almost two years. After my first degree, I became a teaching assistant and still worked at the aforementioned radio station for a year concurrently. In pursuit for higher grounds in my field, I studied for my Masters in Communication studies at Universitas Diponegoro, Indonesia. Studying in a totally different culture broadened my scope of thinking. It made me realize how diverse the world is and how diverse humans can become too. With this broader perspective, I decided to venture into several other disciplines outside my scope. I tried with leadership and became the leader for the international students in the country. In my own small world, I tried to be a home barber and I was successful in styling the hair of my student friends. I also tried in soccer and became the captain of my school team. And now I have fully ventured into Web Development with my all. I therefore understand that, there is nothing we as humans cannot do. It all starts from trying. And the word again is; trying. This is where I developed the mentality of the lion. He is not the smartest, fastest nor the biggest in the jungle but he makes up his mind and goes for the catch. He tries and he succeeeds, he does it over and over again. Then he comes to believe in himself as the king. In so doing, every other being believes that he is indeed the king.
